What is the need of Cage's?

Cages are an important part of keeping pet birds safe and healthy. They provide a secure and comfortable environment for birds to live in, and can protect them from potential dangers such as predators and household hazards. Cages also allow for easy monitoring and care of birds, making it easy for owners to ensure that their feathered friends are getting the proper nutrition, exercise, and medical attention they need.

How often should I clean a perch?

Perches should be cleaned when they are dirty as they may get food or feces on them during the course of a day. Perches can be washed and scrubbed with detergent or disinfectant and should be rinsed thoroughly before using them again.

Why are Cages Important for Birds ?

Cages are important for birds because they provide a safe and secure environment for them to live in. They protect birds from potential dangers such as predators and household hazards. Cages also allow for easy monitoring and care of birds, making it easy for owners to ensure that their feathered friends are getting the proper nutrition, exercise, and medical attention they need. Additionally, cages can be a great way to keep birds entertained and mentally stimulated, with various toys and perches.
